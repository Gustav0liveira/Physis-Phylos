# Hawking Radiation

The metric representation for the stationary Schwarzchild black hole is expressed as

$$
ds^2=-\left(1-\frac{2M}{r}\right)dt^2+\frac{dr^2}{1-\frac{2M}{r}}+r^2\left(d\theta^2+\sin^2\theta d\varphi^2\right)
$$
